This is not a "smile and reassure" role. It's a client-trust role. You'll lead calls, translate performance into clear updates, handle pressure calmly, and make sure clients feel confident about what's happening and what's next.

What You'll Do

  • • Own client communication and calls for your book of accounts
  • • Run weekly or bi-weekly calls with clear structure and next steps
  • • Translate performance into honest, clear client updates
  • • When performance dips: explain calmly with facts, push internally for fixes
  • • Keep timelines, deliverables, and follow-ups clean and tracked
  • • Spot upsell and expansion opportunities and flag them
  • • Collect testimonials and positive feedback at the right moments

This Will Be For You If

  • • You have 3+ years in account management or client success
  • • You're native or fully fluent in English, comfortable leading calls
  • • You're calm under pressure and don't take client stress personally
  • • You're organized, detail-oriented, and move fast on what matters
  • • You don't overpromise to make clients happy. You align internally first.
  • • You're curious about paid ads and want to learn Meta/Google fundamentals
  • • You can handle 15-20 clients without dropping quality

Do Not Apply If

  • • You tend to procrastinate or take too long on time-sensitive issues
  • • You leave client questions unanswered or avoid uncomfortable conversations
  • • You make commitments to clients without internal approval
  • • You're not interested in learning how paid ads and delivery works
  • • You need constant supervision to stay organized

Why Join Us

  • Clear expectations and ownership. You'll know what "good" looks like here.
  • Direct access to leadership. Fast decisions, no corporate layers.
  • High standards, low drama. We care about performance and professionalism.
  • 100% remote. Work from anywhere, 8 AM to 4 PM EST.
