This Role Is For You If...

You don't need to be told twice. You build it right the first time.

You've spent years in operations, probably in an agency or service business. You understand how client delivery works. You know what breaks when a team scales without structure. You have clear opinions about how things should run, and you have the track record to back them up.

Here, you would own the operating rhythm between the CEO and the delivery team: priorities, deadlines, accountability, client delivery quality, ClickUp visibility, and capacity planning. Decisions that don't need the CEO shouldn't reach him.

⚙️

Real Ownership

Not a coordinator role. You own the operation. Your decisions stick.

👥

A Team That Does the Work

Account Managers, Creative Strategists, Media Buyers, Automation Specialist. Professionals. Your job is structure and accountability, not doing everything yourself.

🤖

AI-First Environment

We use AI as a genuine operating layer. You'll have latitude to bring tools, build automations, and shape how the business runs.

📈

Long-Term Role

We hire for permanence. If the fit is right and results are strong, this role grows with the business.

Your mission: free the CEO completely from day-to-day operations so he can stay focused on sales and growth. Client delivery runs with precision. The team always knows what good looks like. Operational issues get resolved before they become client problems.

What You'll Own

  • • Own the delivery rhythm across Account Managers, Creative Strategists, and Media Buyers
  • • Run weekly check-ins and hold performance accountability
  • • Own ClickUp as the single source of truth
  • • Ensure SOPs exist for every repeatable process
  • • Own client delivery escalations and bring the CEO only decision-ready issues when needed
  • • Monitor delivery quality across all accounts
  • • Lead the team's adoption of AI and automation tools
  • • Make data-backed hiring recommendations before capacity breaks

Who You Are

  • • 3-5 years of operations management in an agency or service business
  • • You've managed teams directly or functionally and been accountable for delivery outcomes
  • • You build systems once and they hold
  • • You hold people accountable directly and clearly
  • • You make decisions from data, not gut feelings
  • • You're genuinely AI-first with real examples to prove it
  • • You flag problems before they become fires
  • • Working knowledge of digital marketing and ad delivery cycles

Do Not Apply If

  • • You need every process defined before you can start
  • • You have never directly managed a team and owned their results
  • • You have no experience in an agency or client-facing service business
  • • You are not actively using AI tools in your current work
  • • You cannot commit to 40 hours per week full-time

Why Join Us

  • Full operational authority. Not a support role. You own delivery, team management, and systems. Your judgment is trusted.
  • No corporate layers. Direct access to the CEO. Fast decisions. Clear chain of command.
  • High standards, low drama. We care about performance and building things that last.
  • 100% remote. Async-first communication. No meetings for the sake of meetings.
  • Competitive compensation. Base with performance bonuses as you prove results.
