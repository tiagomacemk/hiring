The role

A practical, hands-on media buying role

We're not looking for someone who only talks in high-level strategy. We need someone who can look inside a Meta account, understand what is happening, spot issues, and make clear decisions.

You should be comfortable with campaign setup, daily optimization, budget adjustments, account structure, performance diagnosis, reporting, and working with a team on new creative tests.

This role is best for someone with solid mid-to-senior level Meta Ads experience, but the public title is simple: Media Buyer.

What you'll do

Manage accounts, diagnose performance, and help us test better creative

Meta account management

Manage campaign setup, optimization, testing, scaling, budget changes, and regular performance checks.

Performance diagnosis

When results drop, you can work through tracking, funnel, audience, creative, budget, CPM, CTR, CVR, and recent account changes logically.

Creative feedback

You understand creative is a major part of Meta performance and can suggest angles, hooks, tests, and learnings based on account data.

This role is for you if...

  • You have real hands-on experience managing Meta Ads accounts.
  • You can explain the exact decisions you made and why.
  • You have worked with ecommerce, lead generation, coaching/consulting, info products, or service-based accounts.
  • You know how to read performance beyond surface-level ROAS screenshots.
  • You are analytical, structured, proactive, and reliable.
  • You can communicate clearly with a remote team.

Do not apply if...

  • You only have course knowledge and little hands-on account experience.
  • You cannot show or explain specific results from accounts you worked on.
  • You need someone else to tell you every small optimization to make.
  • You treat creative testing as someone else's problem.
  • You are unavailable when urgent account issues happen.
  • You give vague answers instead of specifics.
