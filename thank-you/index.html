Frequently asked questions

What happens on the strategy call?

We review your numbers, acquisition, creative, and biggest bottleneck. You leave with clear priorities, whether or not we work together.

What can Macedo Marketing help with?

Meta ads, creative strategy and production, account audits, testing systems, and ongoing optimization. We add Google Ads when it supports the wider acquisition plan.

Do I need a certain level of ad spend?

There is no fixed minimum. Consistent spend and conversion data make the call more useful, but fit also depends on margins, offer, and growth goals.

Are results guaranteed?

No. These are verified client outcomes, not promises. Results depend on the product, offer, economics, creative, market, and execution.

What happens if we are a fit?

We agree on the plan, scope, and onboarding steps. The call creates no obligation to move forward.
