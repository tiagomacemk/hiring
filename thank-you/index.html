Case study 01Health ecommerce
$0 → $282K+/mo

A compliant growth engine built from scratch.

Sofia Alonso · Elara

A regulated health launch had no account history, no proven creative, and no room for sloppy compliance. We built the acquisition system before trying to force scale.

Starting point
  • Launching from zero with no historical conversion data
  • CAC had reached $200
  • Healthcare restrictions limited messaging and creative options
What changed
  • Built compliant Meta account and tracking infrastructure
  • Tested creative around real objections instead of generic benefits
  • Reworked the purchase journey from ad click to checkout

  • $282K+ monthly revenue by month three
  • $17.7K in ad spend
  • CAC reduced to $60.70
Case study 02Digital education
£30K → £100K+/mo

More booked calls, stronger intent, cleaner scale.

Vijay Singh · Talking Houses

Lead volume looked healthy, but broken tracking made qualified demand impossible to separate from noise. We fixed attribution first, then scaled the formats and funnel that produced real sales conversations.

Starting point
  • Monthly revenue around £30K
  • 66 booked calls but unreliable attribution
  • No dependable signal for qualified demand
What changed
  • Rebuilt tracking to see which campaigns created revenue
  • Optimized toward high-intent actions, not cheap leads
  • Tested long- and short-form video before moving to a webinar funnel
Outcome
  • £100K+ monthly revenue within six months
  • 363 booked calls
  • £25K in monthly ad spend at scale
Case study 03Digital fitness
$106K in 90 days

Turning a low-ROAS account into a testing system.

Rory Tonkin · Hard To Kill Fitness

Several agencies had already failed to push the account beyond 1.2 ROAS. The fix was not another audience hack. It was a disciplined testing and retargeting system.

Starting point
  • Multiple agencies had failed to scale the account profitably
  • ROAS had stalled around 1.2
  • No repeatable structure for testing or scaling
What changed
  • Audited the campaign structure, funnel, and tracking
  • Separated creative tests from scaling campaigns
  • Retargeted prospects by awareness and traffic temperature
Outcome
  • $106K in revenue within 90 days
  • Cost per acquisition reduced by 54%
  • A repeatable system for testing and scaling

Frequently asked questions

What happens on the strategy call?

We look at your current growth goals, paid acquisition, creative testing, and the bottleneck holding performance back. You leave with clearer next steps, whether or not we work together.

Who is the call best suited for?

Ecommerce, DTC, and digital-product brands that already have a validated offer and want to improve paid-media performance, creative output, or profitable scale.

What can Macedo Marketing help with?

Meta ads management, creative strategy and production, account audits, testing systems, and ongoing optimization. Google Ads can be included where it supports the wider acquisition plan.

Do I need a certain level of ad spend?

Consistent spend and enough conversion data make the call more useful, but fit depends on the business, margins, offer, and growth goal. If it is too early, we will tell you directly.

Are results guaranteed?

No responsible agency can guarantee a specific revenue or ROAS outcome. Performance depends on the product, offer, economics, creative, market, and execution. The results shown above are real client outcomes, not promises of future performance.

What happens if we are a fit?

We outline the recommended plan, scope, and onboarding steps. The call itself creates no obligation to move forward.
